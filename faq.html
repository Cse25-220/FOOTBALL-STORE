📦 Orders & Payments

How do I place an order? ▼
Simply browse our shop, click "Add to Cart" on any product you like, then head to your cart and click "Proceed to Checkout". You can pay securely by card, PayPal, or Apple Pay.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it? ▼
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of being placed. After that, they may have already been dispatched. Contact us as soon as possible at support@kickzone.co.uk.
How do I use a coupon code? ▼
Enter your coupon code in the "Coupon Code" box on the cart page and click "Apply". The discount will be applied immediately to your order total.
Is my payment information secure? ▼
Absolutely. We use Stripe for payment processing — your card details are never stored on our servers. All data is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.

🚚 Delivery & Shipping

How long does delivery take? ▼
Standard delivery takes 2–3 working days. Express next-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2pm Monday–Friday. International shipping typically takes 5–10 working days.
Do you offer free delivery? ▼
Yes! We offer free standard delivery on all UK orders over £50. Orders under £50 have a flat delivery fee of £4.99.
Do you ship internationally? ▼
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International delivery costs vary by destination and are calculated at checkout. Import duties may apply depending on your country.
How do I track my order? ▼
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number by email. You can track your parcel directly via our courier's website. Alternatively, log into your KickZone account to view your order status.

↩️ Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy? ▼
We offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn, unwashed, and unmodified items in their original packaging. Simply contact us to initiate a return and we'll send you a prepaid return label.
How long do refunds take? ▼
Once we receive your returned item and inspect it, your refund will be processed within 3–5 working days. It may take a further 5–10 days to appear in your bank account depending on your provider.
What if I received a wrong or damaged item? ▼
We're very sorry if this happened! Please contact us within 48 hours with photos of the item and your order number. We'll send a replacement immediately and arrange collection of the incorrect item at no cost to you.
Can I exchange an item for a different size? ▼
Yes! We offer free exchanges for size issues. Contact our team within 30 days of receiving your order and we'll arrange the swap. Stock permitting, we'll dispatch the new size the same day we receive your return.

⚽ Products & Sizing

Are your shirts and boots 100% authentic? ▼
Absolutely. Every product we sell is sourced directly from official brand distributors and comes with the relevant authenticity tags and certificates. We never stock replicas or counterfeit items — guaranteed.
How do I find my correct shirt size? ▼
Each product page includes a full size guide with chest and waist measurements. As a general rule, football shirts run slightly slim. If you're between sizes or prefer a looser fit, we recommend sizing up.
What football boot type should I choose? ▼
FG (Firm Ground) suits natural grass pitches. AG (Artificial Ground) is for 3G/4G astro turf. SG (Soft Ground) is for muddy pitches with removable studs. TF (Turf) is for hard artificial surfaces. Check the product description for the recommended surface.
Can I get shirt printing/personalisation? ▼
Yes! We offer official name and number printing on most shirts. Simply select the customisation option on the product page before adding to cart. Please note that personalised items cannot be returned unless faulty.

👤 Account & Loyalty

Do I need an account to shop? ▼
No — you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account lets you track orders, save your address, access exclusive deals, and earn loyalty points on every purchase.
How does the KickZone loyalty programme work? ▼
Earn 1 point for every £1 spent. 100 points = £1 off your next order. Points never expire and can be redeemed at checkout. Sign up for a free account to start earning today.
Do you offer a student discount? ▼
Yes! We offer 10% off for students verified through Student Beans or UNiDAYS. Visit the student discount page and verify your student status to receive your unique discount code.
I forgot my password — how do I reset it? ▼
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive promptly.
